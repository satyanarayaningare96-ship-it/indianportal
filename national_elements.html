National Flag

The National Flag is ahorizontal tricolor of India saffron(kesaria)at the top,white in the middle and India green at bottom in the equal portion

National Anthem

The song Jan gan man, composed originally in bangala by Rabindranath Tagore,was adopted in its hindi version by theConstituent Assembly as the Nathional Anthem of India on January 24, 1950.

National Song

The song Vande Mataram, composed in Sanskrit by Bankimchandra Chatterji, was a source of inspiration to the people in their struggle for freedom. It has an equal status with Jana-gana-mana.
